Harmony Meadows: Cutie Quest

Instructions

Welcome to Harmony Meadows: Cutie Quest! In this game, you will play mini-games to find out YOUR own Cutie Mark! Pick your pony, start the game, and use WASD to walk up to ponies to start the game. Complete all 6 quests for your personalized Cutie Mark.
